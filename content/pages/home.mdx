<Section anchor={{ num: "02", label: "Why" }}>
  <h2 className="display-section"><>Why subcellular spatial biology data</></h2>

  <p className="prose-lead mt-8 max-w-[480px]"><>Biological AI today is trained on omics data; which molecules are present, at what levels. These methods don't resolve where molecules are located, or how they're organized in three dimensions.</></p>

  <p className="prose-body mt-12 max-w-[520px]"><>Cellular function depends on subcellular organization: how organelles are arranged, how membranes are shaped, how molecular complexes are positioned. This structural layer exists in small amounts from individual laboratories but not at the massive-scale foundation models require.</></p>

  <p className="prose-body mt-2 max-w-[520px]" style={{ color: "var(--ink)", fontWeight: 500 }}><>We're generating it.</></p>
